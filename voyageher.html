Voyage-HER

An all female travel responsive web app that provides users a safe space to research travel attractions, utilize an exclusive social media feed for sharing photos and videos and access safety tips from fellow female travelers around the globe. This app includes all individuals who identify as female.

Competitive Analysis

Two popular travel apps were studied to gain insight into the current market: Foursquare & NomadHer. I took note positive of UI / UX aspects in the apps such as user friendly naviagational elements, information sorted by category, user review along with others to incoporate in Voyage-HER. Other elements such as invasive sign up processes, grammatical errors and information overload in were noted in the analysis to ensure none of these aspects were part of the final Voyage-HER product.

User Interviews

User were conducted and were invaluable for continuing in the design process allowing greater perspective and user input. Features such as: the importance of ratings and reviews from other travelers, photos and video sharing capability and a clear and simple interface were mentioned by multiple users and emphasized as some of the most important features. A "meetup" feature that was originally part of the product designs but was now put on the backburner as it was of less importance to the users.
